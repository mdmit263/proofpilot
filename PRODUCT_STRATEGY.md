# DueProof Product Strategy

## One-Line Pitch
DueProof is an obligation-defense app that catches deadlines, charges, renewals, forms, and follow-ups before they become expensive problems.

## Why This Can Be Big
Most people do not need another chat app. They need a system that watches their messy life admin and turns it into action.

The daily pain is universal:
- missed bills
- forgotten returns
- surprise subscriptions
- confusing notices
- appointment prep
- school and family forms
- warranties and deadlines
- government and work paperwork

## Differentiation
ChatGPT answers when asked. DueProof keeps a structured radar of obligations, money risk, deadlines, owners, missing information, and resolution status.

The product moat is:
1. Capture-first workflow
2. Personal obligation memory
3. Deadline and money-risk graph
4. Action templates
5. Reminders and follow-through
6. Family and household operating layer

## MVP Features Built
1. Capture messy text
2. Auto-detect category
3. Extract dates, amounts, and references
4. Risk score obligations
5. Money leak dashboard
6. Daily mission list
7. Proof vault
8. Owner routing
9. Action plan generation
10. Mobile installable PWA setup
